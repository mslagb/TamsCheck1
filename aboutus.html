During our research, we observed the struggles that many FEU Roosevelt students and their guardians face when buying uniforms and merchandise. One of the biggest issues? Going to the physical store only to find out that items are out of stockâ€”a frustrating and time-consuming experience. We know this struggle firsthand, and it motivated us to develop a reliable and accessible solution just for FEU Roosevelt students. Our mission is to make the shopping experience easier, faster, and more convenient for every Tamaraw. Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope it helps make your uniform and merchandise shopping hassle-free!
